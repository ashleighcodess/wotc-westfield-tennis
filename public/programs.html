Competitive Play

Tennis Leagues

Put your skills to the test in our women's leagues. Weekly matches, standings, and end-of-season playoffs make for exciting competition.

Find Your Level

We organize leagues by NTRP rating to ensure competitive, enjoyable matches. Not sure of your rating? Our pros can help assess your level.

Each season runs 8 weeks with matches scheduled at convenient times. League fees include court time, balls, and prizes for division winners.

2.5

Social League

Perfect for newer players developing consistency and learning match strategy.

3.0

Intermediate League

For players with reliable strokes who are comfortable in match situations.

3.5

Advanced League

Competitive play for experienced players with strong all-around games.

4.0+

Championship League

Our most competitive division for tournament-level players.
