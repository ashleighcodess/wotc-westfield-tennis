Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about joining our community.

Do I need to be a club member to join?

No! Tennis Together is open to everyone, whether or not you're a member of Westfield Indoor Tennis Club. Some events may have different pricing for members vs. non-members.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

We welcome all skill levels! Our beginner program is perfect for those new to tennis, and we offer clinics and events specifically designed for players just starting out.

How do I sign up for events?

Simply visit our Events page and click the "Register" button for any event you're interested in. Payment is handled securely through Stripe.

Can I bring a friend to try it out?

Absolutely! We love when members bring friends. Many of our social events are perfect for first-timers to experience the community.

What should I bring to my first event?

Just bring yourself, comfortable athletic clothing, and tennis shoes. We have rackets available to borrow if you need one!

Is there parking available?

Yes! There's a parking lot adjacent to the club, plus street parking available on E Broad St. Parking is free for club visitors.
