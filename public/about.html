Our Story

Welcome to Tennis Together

Tennis Together was born from a simple idea: tennis is better together. What started as a small group of friends meeting for weekly matches has grown into a thriving community of players who support, challenge, and inspire each other on and off the court.

Based at the historic Westfield Indoor Tennis Club, we've created a space where players of all skill levels can come together to improve their game, make lasting friendships, and enjoy the sport we love.

"Our mission is to make tennis accessible, enjoyable, and empowering for everyone in our community. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or you've been playing for decades, there's a place for you here."

From competitive leagues to casual clinics, social events to skill-building workshops, we offer something for everyone. Join us and discover why our members call this community their second home.

55+ Years of Tennis

What We Stand For

Our community is built on three core pillars that guide everything we do.

Community

We believe in the power of connection. Our members form lasting friendships that extend far beyond the tennis court, supporting each other through life's ups and downs.

Empowerment

Tennis builds confidence, resilience, and strength. We're here to help every player discover their potential and celebrate their progress, no matter where they start.

Wellness

Physical activity, mental clarity, and social connection all contribute to a healthy, balanced life. Tennis gives us all three in one beautiful sport.
