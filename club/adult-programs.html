Adult Programs

Group lessons, competitive leagues, and private instruction for every level.

Class Levels

Find Your Level

Our programs are organized by skill level so you always play with the right group.

Novice

Covering fundamentals with no experience required. Perfect for those brand new to tennis.

Advanced Beginner / Low Intermediate

Emphasizing movement and baseline consistency. Building on the basics with more dynamic play.

Advanced Intermediate / Team 3.0, 3.5

Focusing on competitive strategy, point construction, and match play situations.

Advanced

For former top-level high school and college players. High-intensity drills and competitive play.
