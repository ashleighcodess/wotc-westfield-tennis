Our Story

Over 55 Years of Tennis in Westfield

Westfield Indoor Tennis Club has been a cornerstone of the Westfield community since 1969. As an independently operated and owned family business for over 50 years, we take pride in providing quality tennis instruction and facilities for players of all ages and skill levels.

We serve both junior and adult populations with programs ranging from introductory lessons for children as young as 4 to competitive league play for experienced adults. Our offerings include contract court time, racket sales and restringing, group and private lessons, men's ladder, USTA adult leagues, and Central Jersey women's traveling teams.

For availability information, please email us or call (908) 233-9480.

Club Policies

Payment & Registration

  • Cash or check only — make checks payable to "ACES." No credit cards accepted.
  • No refunds on program registration fees.
  • Pre-paid private instruction is mandatory for all prime-time lessons (weekdays 3:00pm–10:00pm and weekends).

Make-Up & Cancellation

  • There are no make-ups for missed classes.
  • Limited exceptions: with 24-hour advance notice and an available spot in an alternative class.
  • Maximum of 2 make-ups allowed per session.
  • Court rental cancellations must be phoned in at least 24 hours in advance.
