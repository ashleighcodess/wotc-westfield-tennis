Reservation Policy

  • Courts may be reserved starting one week prior to the scheduled court date.
  • Half-hour increments available (30, 60, 90, 120 minutes, etc.).
  • Cancellations must be phoned in at least 24 hours in advance.
  • We accept cash or checks only, please. Make checks payable to "ACES."
  • Friday nights open until 10:00pm.
